Replacement Policy

Thank you for shopping at Afia Borka House. This Replacement Policy explains when and how you can request a replacement for products purchased from our online store or physical outlets.

1. Overview

Our goal is to ensure a smooth and trustworthy shopping experience. If you receive a product that is damaged, defective, mismatched, or not as described, you may be eligible to request a replacement, subject to the terms and conditions below.

2. Eligibility for Replacement

You may request a replacement under the following circumstances:

  • Product received in damaged or broken condition.
  • Product has a manufacturing defect (stitching, fabric, zipper, button, accessories, etc.).
  • You received a wrong item, size, color, or design compared to your order.
  • Product received is incomplete (missing pieces from sets or bundled items).

All replacement requests must be raised within 48 hours of receiving the product, based on delivery confirmation.

3. Non-Replaceable Items

The following items are generally not eligible for replacement, unless they are damaged or defective:

  • Innerwear, undergarments, socks, and hygiene-sensitive items.
  • Personal care, cosmetics, and beauty products that have been opened or used.
  • Products bought during clearance, final sale, or special promotion where replacement is not mentioned.
  • Items with tags removed, washed, altered, or used after delivery.
  • Products damaged due to customer misuse, negligence, or improper care.

4. Replacement Request Process

To request a replacement, please follow these steps:

  1. Take clear photos of the product, packaging, invoice, and the issue (damage/defect/wrong item).
  2. Contact our support team via phone, email, or messaging channel within 48 hours of delivery.
  3. Provide order details including Order ID, customer name, contact number, and delivery address.
  4. Our team will review your request and may ask for additional information or photos if needed.
  5. Once approved, you will receive instructions for returning the product or arranging a pickup (where available).

5. Replacement Timeline & Shipping

  • After we receive and inspect the returned product, the replacement will typically be processed within 3–7 working days.
  • If the requested size/color is out of stock, we may offer an alternative product of similar value or a store credit, as per company policy.
  • In cases where the issue is confirmed to be from our side (wrong item, manufacturing defect, damage in transit), return shipping costs may be borne by us.
  • In other scenarios, return or re-delivery charges may be deducted or charged as applicable.

6. Product Condition Requirements

For a successful replacement, please ensure that:

  • The product is returned in its original packaging with tags, labels, and accessories intact.
  • The item has not been used, washed, ironed, or altered in any way.
  • The original invoice or proof of purchase is included in the return.
  • The product is securely packed to avoid further damage during return transit.
